Why Choose Us!

Why You Should Trust Us? Learn More About Us!

Founded in 2016, Twiggle CAD Studio is a global provider of professional photo editing and floor plan solutions for the real estate and commercial property industries. Based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, we proudly support clients across America, Europe, Asia, and Australia..

Design Approach

We blend creative design with engineering precision to deliver floor plans that are both beautiful and buildable, ensuring your project stays on time and on budget.

Innovative Solutions

We leverage advanced CAD tools to produce accurate, data-driven deliverables that minimize revisions and accelerate stakeholder approvals.

Project Management

Our structured delivery process keeps revisions tracked, communication clear, and deadlines met—so you always know the status of your project.
