About Us

A Creative Architecture Agency For Your Dream Home

Founded in 2016, Twiggle CAD Studio is a global provider of professional photo editing and floor plan solutions for the real estate and commercial property industries. Based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, we proudly support clients across America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.

Our strategic location allows us to work efficiently across international time zones while drawing on a team of highly skilled, English-speaking CAD professionals. This ensures fast turnaround times, consistent quality, and cost-effective pricing that helps protect our clients’ margins.
